about

Today, Jumeirah is a Dubai national
champion and an acclaimed luxury hospitality leader — operating 24
Jumeirah branded properties (6,747 keys) in eight countries. As exemplified
through its flagship hotel, and global icon, Burj Al Arab, Jumeirah
has transformed sthe way that luxury is defined.

In 2018, Jumeirah entered a phase of
accelerated growth under the leadership
of its new CEO, José Silva. We have seen unparalleled success
in Dubai and regionally, and will build upon
this expertise as it expands internationally.
In terms of strategy, Jumeirah Group will continue
to own properties and grow through
acquisition, as well as hotel management agreements. Both new and
existing markets
have been identified for expansion
across Middle East, Europe and Asia with the
vision to take the brand global — with each property clearly stamped with the
Jumeirah signature, to make a strong statement within the industry.
